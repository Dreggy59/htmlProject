Main Cast of Avengers

Iron Man

Tony Stark, the genius who started it all, faces the ultimate test of his legacy, sacrificing everything to save the universe.

Spider Man

Peter Parker returns from the snap, eager to help his mentor and fellow heroes in the final battle, driven by hope and the desire to make a difference in saving the universe.

Black Widow

Natasha Romanoff’s journey comes full circle as she makes the greatest sacrifice, proving her loyalty and love for her found family.

Hawkeye

Clint Barton, now a broken man, is driven by vengeance and redemption, fighting to bring back what he lost after the snap.

Thor

The God of Thunder struggles with guilt and failure, rediscovering his worth as he fights alongside his friends to save the universe.

Hulk

Bruce Banner finally balances brains and brawn, becoming a hero who unites science and strength to reverse the devastation of the snap.

Doctor Strange

Stephen Strange holds the key to a single chance of victory, guiding the heroes from beyond the snap’s reach.

Rocket

Rocket, the survivor of his fractured family, rises as a leader, using wit and firepower to defend the universe alongside his new team.

Captain America

Steve Rogers, the unwavering leader, faces his destiny with courage and honor, choosing to finally live the life he fought so hard to protect.

Ant Man

Scott Lang, the unlikely hero, plays a pivotal role in time-travel adventures, proving that even the smallest hero can make a huge difference.

Nebula

Nebula battles her past and chooses her own path, defying her former self to become a savior in the fight against her father, Thanos.

War Machine

James Rhodes stands strong in his armored suit, defending his allies and proving that every hero matters in the battle for the universe.
